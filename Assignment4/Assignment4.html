Purpose of this site:

This site was created to help struggling university students with basic citations in the most common formats. Citations are important to maintaining academic integrity. If citations are not done properly, plagiarism will occur.

To get started:

All you have to do is click one of the links on the sidebar which will take you to the citation machine of your choosing. If you decide to switch citation methods or desire to return to this page, simply click the desired link on the navigation bar when you get to the page.
